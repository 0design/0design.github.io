About the project

Glance is an app which aims to use the data you provide and on your phone, to provide you with relevant information and updates. We redesigned their entire android app in a hybrid fashion which incorporated the core elements of Material design but also catered to the unique interactions and visual feel they had in mind. The illustrations made for the onboarding all followed the material style. The app targeted a large Hindi audience so we had to make sure that the visual design of the app had the same feel during both English and Hindi use. For this we used the font Ek Mukta which provides uniformity across Hindi and English. The app also had a unique flip interaction used by its cards which allow the user to just tap and view information. We designed the cards in a way to maximise the use of this interaction, taking inspiration from how Google designs its cards.
